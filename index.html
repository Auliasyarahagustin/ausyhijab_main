About Us

In our first year, Ausy Hijab was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create high-quality, stylish hijabs that empower women to express their unique identities. We started as a small, passionate team committed to sourcing the best fabrics and crafting hijabs that combine comfort with elegance. Our initial collection was met with enthusiastic response, confirming our belief in the need for fashionable and functional hijabs.

  • 2009-2011

    Expanding Horizons

    Building on our early success, our second year focused on expanding our product range and reaching a broader audience. We introduced new collections, including sports hijabs and pashminas, catering to diverse needs and preferences. Our team grew, and we began to establish a presence in both local and international markets. The positive feedback and loyal customer base motivated us to keep innovating and improving.

  • March 2019

    Innovating for You

    By our third year, Ausy Hijab became known for its innovative designs and high standards. We launched the Ausy Instant Pashmina Hijab, a game-changer for busy women who want effortless style. This year also saw the introduction of our patterned and shimmer collections, adding variety and sparkle to our offerings. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remained unwavering as we continued to refine our products and services.

  • December 2020

    Strengthening Community

    In our fourth year, we focused on building a strong community around the Ausy Hijab brand. We launched various initiatives to connect with our customers, including social media campaigns, styling workshops, and community events. We also started collaborating with influencers and fashion experts to inspire and guide our customers in styling their hijabs. Our customer-centric approach helped us build a loyal and engaged community.

  • July 2022

    Looking Ahead

    As we celebrate our fifth year, Ausy Hijab is proud of our journey and excited for the future. We continue to innovate, with plans to introduce new materials and designs that cater to the evolving needs of our customers. Our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices is stronger than ever, as we aim to make a positive impact on both our community and the environment. We are grateful for the support of our customers and look forward to many more years of growth and success together.
